Zero-Tolerance Policy on Child Sexual Abuse & Exploitation (CSAE)

VibeCheck strictly forbids any content that exploits or endangers minors. We remove such material immediately, terminate offending accounts, and report all cases to the appropriate authorities.


1 Prohibited content
  • Any sexual content involving minors, including drawings, audio, or text.
  • Requests for sexual content involving minors.
  • Accounts that solicit, distribute, or create CSAE material.

2 Detection & removal

We use human review to detect CSAE. Detected material is:

  • Immediately removed from our servers.
  • Logged and preserved for law-enforcement requests.
  • Reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant authorities.

3 User reporting

To report suspected CSAE content or behavior:

  • Email nugsagiri@gmail.com with the subject “Urgent – CSAE Report”.
  • Include the username(s), screenshots, and any relevant details.
  • We review and act on all CSAE reports within 7 days.

4 Account termination

Any user involved in CSAE will be permanently banned. Devices and IPs may also be blocked.


5 Law-enforcement cooperation

We cooperate fully with law-enforcement agencies, promptly providing required data under applicable laws.
